BULLS AND COWS

How to play

You have to find the four digit number. Each try you are given a clue how close you are, the clues are in the form of bulls and cows. Each cow means that you found one of the digits and each bull mean that you found the digit and it's position, also bulls are not counted as cows too, what this means is that if you find a digit and it's position you will get a bull, not a bull and a cow.

Points

The points are calculated based on the number of guesses and the time it took you to guess the number. Smaller number means better, just like in golf. Points are used so you can see how good you performed and for the leadeboard.
